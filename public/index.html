Tired of going to the gym with no purpose, if so, this challenge is for you. This is a challenge that test complete fitness no matter strength or weight. In this challenge, the participants will bench press 75% body weight, squat 85% of body, and run a 2.5K (Women bench 50% and squat 65%). Your score will be calculated by taking 1min off your 2.5K for every 10 reps performed on your total bench press and squat. The lower your time or score, the better and how the winners are chosen.
